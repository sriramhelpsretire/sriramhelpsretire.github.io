About this site

Welcome to a peaceful place where we discuss every aspect of retirement. The hope is to make you feel at peace financially — and retire comfortably.

The philosophy that you don't retire out of something, but into something emphasizes that retirement is a transition to a new phase with a bigger purpose.

Retirement Planner — A Pocket Guide is a compendium of information and calculations to help plan your retirement. Retirement is not all about finance; but once you secure your finances, you can plan all the good things you want to get into.

📖
Educational Insights Simplified breakdowns of Social Security, Medicare, tax strategy, and more.
🧮
Interactive Tools Hands-on calculators to model your specific financial future — with more added regularly.

Healthcare Planning

Healthcare in retirement is a two-chapter story: before 65 (ACA marketplace or COBRA) and after 65 (Medicare Parts A, B, D and Medigap). Getting this wrong is costly — plan early.
