"What if" Analysis using this Calculator

📝 We promised earlier that finance planning which is one of the pillars of retirement planning is complicated and often times never looked at closely. It is a multi-dimensional problem where each of the factors play a major role in adding different color to your retirement landscape. There is no "one size fits all" approach to planning financial projection and planning arount it
📝 Retirement Portfolio Calculator is an extensive calculator (more features are getting added frequently). It is meant to give a set of inputs to be provided by the user on the left. You can look at your "current financial situation" and enter those values in appropriate boxes. Once it is done you hit "Calculate Projection" and the tool does its job of taking the values, working with various aspects including growth, tax etc and forecasts your financial landscape based on certain assumptions which are also driven by the user.
📝 You can choose to be conservative or aggressive and try to get some meaningful interpretation of the data you entered, to know your financial position year by year till you reach age 90 We can add more years and it should not be conceived as this calculator took life expectancy type of parameters. Age 90 is arbitrary for calculations.
📝 Retirement Portfolio Calculator gives you an idea of how your invested money grows in various assets. It is vital to understand and plan such buckets so you can keep your cash flow clear. Each year in retirement could be different, so you need to run the numbers to plan where it will come from.
