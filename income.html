💰 Income Planning

Foundation Layer
Income Categories
  • Salary (base pay)
  • Bonuses (variable)
  • Side income (consulting, freelancing)
  • Equity compensation (RSU, ESPP)
  • Passive income (rent, dividends, interest)
What to DO during working years

A. Stabilize + Grow Income

  • Increase primary income every 2–3 years
  • Build at least one secondary income stream
  • Avoid over-reliance on bonuses

B. Convert Income → Assets

Income is temporary, assets are permanent

  • Target savings rate: 25%–40%+
  • Automate:
    • 401(k)
    • HSA
    • Brokerage

C. Smooth Income Volatility

  • Treat bonuses as:
    • 50% invest
    • 25% goals
    • 25% lifestyle (optional)

D. Track since early years

  • Fixed vs variable income
  • Growth rate assumption
  • Income end age


💼 Salary (Base Pay)

What it is

  • Fixed compensation paid regularly (monthly/bi-weekly)
  • Most predictable and stable component of income
  • Forms the foundation for financial planning

🔑 Why Salary is Critical

  • Drives bonus calculations (% of base)
  • Determines retirement contributions (401k %)
  • Impacts Social Security benefits
  • Affects borrowing capacity
  • Influences tax bracket positioning
