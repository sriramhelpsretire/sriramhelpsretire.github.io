// Liquidity & Longevity Planning

This is a very important area where we are discussing how long your money is expected to last.
As we discussed earlier, it is essential to save money, invest, take care of asset allocation, and plan for cash flow. We must accurately figure out how cash is made available for various expenses.
This includes necessary tax payments, monthly recurring expenses, and discretionary spending (travel, etc.). Once Social Security income begins, balancing and re-allocation must be adjusted to account for all various sources of retirement income.
The primary purpose of this specific calculation is to let the user input their desired beginning cash value and projected annual withdrawal (required based on other financial planning calculations) and forecast precisely how long it would last.
