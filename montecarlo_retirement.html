Ages & Contributions

Inflation-adjusted each year. Phase 1: from Brokerage+Bonds. Phase 2: SS covers first, shortfall from Brokerage+Bonds then 401k. Phase 3: SS+RMD covers spending+tax, surplus back to Brokerage.

Social Security

Wife SS starts at her age 67 (FRA), at 50% of husband's amount. Both grow with COLA (= inflation rate) each year.

Tax Rate

Applied to taxable SS (85%) + RMD in Phase 3. Applied to taxable SS (85%) in Phase 2. No tax on brokerage draws (after-tax money).
